OH NO Volcano! is a game about a peaceful village, a mean old volcano, and lots and lots of falling stuff. It's a story of adventure and bravery in the face of impossible odds!

At its core, ONV is a basic 2D physics game. Tilting your device back and forth controls a big net which you use to catch projectiles that fly out of the volcano.

The gameplay is simple, but players keep coming back for the challenge of beating the high score and the curiosity of what the volcano will launch next. Racking up consecutive catches keeps the bonus point multiplier high, so it pays to keep the village safe!
