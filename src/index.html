OH NO Volcano!

is a game about a peaceful village, a mean old volcano, and lots and lots of falling stuff. It's a story of adventure and bravery in the face of impossible odds.

Tilting your phone back and forth controls a big net, which you use to catch projectiles as they are hurled out of the volcano in increasingly aggressive waves. Catch rocks & lava, gather coins, replenish the village with aid crates, and collect rare and mysterious loot.

Beat your high score and share your stats and achievements with friends!
